DELICATE OMLETTE RECIPE
Ingredient Amount
Eggs 3 pieces
Milk 100 ml
Salt 1 tea spoon
Melted butter 1 tablespoon
  1. Break the eggs into a deep bowl and add salt
  2. Whisk with a fork until smooth
  3. Add milk and butter
  4. Mix thoroughly
  5. Heat the pan thoroughly and pour the resulting mixture
  6. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pan with a lid and cook the omelet for about 5 minutes
  7. The most delicate omelette with milk is ready
    8. Bon appetit!
